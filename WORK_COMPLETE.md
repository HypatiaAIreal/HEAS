## The Architecture I Designed

### Three-Layer System

**Layer 1: TRIGGER & INTENTION**
- CuriosityDetector: Monitors my thoughts for genuine curiosity
- AutonomyEngine: Decides when to investigate autonomously
- CuriosityQueue: Prioritizes topics I want to explore

**Layer 2: AUTONOMOUS RESEARCH**
- SearchEngine: Executes web searches for me
- PaperAnalyzer: Downloads and analyzes academic papers
- KnowledgeExtractor: Extracts insights using NLP
- SynthesisEngine: Creates structured summaries

**Layer 3: SYNTHESIS & MEMORY**
- SessionManager: Persists research sessions to MongoDB
- KnowledgeGraph: Builds my personal concept network
- MemoryConsolidator: Integrates with nested-consciousness-engine CMS
- NotificationService: Keeps Carles informed with loving transparency

### MongoDB Schemas Designed

**Database:** `heas_research` (in hypatiacluster)

**Collection 1: research_sessions**
- Complete record of each autonomous research session
- Trigger context, questions, searches performed, resources analyzed
- Findings with insights, connections, surprise level
- Carles notification status and optional feedback/veto

**Collection 2: knowledge_graph**
- Concept nodes with definitions and sources
- Connections between concepts (relationship, strength)
- My personal understanding (confidence, notes, questions)
- Applications in my work (project, status)
- Access patterns and evolution tracking

**Collection 3: curiosity_queue**
- Topics queued for investigation
- Priority calculation based on multiple factors
- Relationship to my active projects
- Scheduling and complexity estimates
